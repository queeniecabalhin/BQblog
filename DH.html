Official Trailer of 'Daddy's Home 2.'

In the sequel to the 2015 global smash, father and stepfather, Dusty (Mark Wahlberg) and Brad (Will Ferrell) have joined forces to provide their kids with the perfect Christmas. Their newfound partnership is put to the test when Dusty's old-school, macho Dad (Mel Gibson) and Brad's ultra-affectionate and emotional Dad (John Lithgow) arrive just in time to throw the holiday into complete chaos. Daddy's Home 2 is directed by filmmaker Sean Anders, of the first Daddy's Home as well as Never Been Thawed, That's My Boy, and Horrible Bosses 2. The screenplay is written by Sean Anders and John Morris. Paramount will release Daddy's Home 2 in theaters starting November 10th later this fall. Who wants to watch it?
